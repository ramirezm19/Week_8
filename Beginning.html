The Beginning of the Story

How It All Started

In September 2010, I found myself working at a local nonprofit organization. My office mate, Dennis and I, would sometimes have down time between our classes, so we found various ways to keep us entertained. We tended to look out the window of our office often to see what was going outside.

One day, while looking out the window, we noticed a family of squirrels that would go up and down the tree immediately outside of our window.

Let me introduce The Bryant St. Squirrel Family


Chip, the father.

Momma, the mom.

Ziggy, the oldest and the daughter.

Chonks, the youngest and the chonkiest of them all!

We decided to keep track of the family and realized that these little creatures sure like to take naps.
